Getting Started

Welcome to Chess Tournament Manager!

Our platform makes it easy to participate in and organize chess tournaments. Here's how to get started:

1. Complete Your Profile

Go to Profile settings and add your chess rating, preferred time controls, and contact information.

2. Browse Tournaments

Use the "Join Tournament" feature to find tournaments that match your skill level and schedule.

3. Register & Play

Register for tournaments and receive notifications about pairings and schedules.

Tournament Guide

Joining Tournaments

  • Browse available tournaments by format, time control, and skill level
  • Check tournament details including entry fee, prize pool, and schedule
  • Register before the deadline
  • Receive pairing notifications via email

Creating Tournaments

  • Set tournament format (Swiss, Round Robin, Knockout)
  • Define time controls and increment settings
  • Set player limits and entry requirements
  • Configure prizes and entry fees
  • Manage registrations and generate pairings

Tournament Management

  • Enter game results as they are completed
  • Generate and publish standings
  • Handle disputes and adjournments
  • Distribute prizes and ratings updates

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a tournament?

Click "Join Tournament" on the main page, browse available tournaments, and click "Register" for your chosen tournament before the deadline.

Can I withdraw from a tournament?

Yes, you can withdraw before the first round begins. Go to your Tournament History and click "Withdraw" next to the tournament.

How are pairings determined?

Pairings follow standard tournament rules (Swiss system by default) and are generated automatically by our system.

What if I can't make my scheduled game?

Contact the tournament director immediately. Depending on the tournament rules, games may be rescheduled or result in a forfeit.

How do I report a game result?

Both players can enter results through their tournament dashboard. Results are confirmed when both players agree or a tournament director verifies.
